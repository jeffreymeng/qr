Welcome to QRID

A qr reading and writing service mainly intended for internal development and use only, but you're still welcome to use it if you want.

Documentation:

To generate a qr code, include a iframe with the following url: 

https://qr.jeffkmeng.com/generate?params

Parameters:
Parameter Description Defualt
data The data that the qr code should show. required
size The legnth of one side of the qr code(it is a square) 128
colorDark The dark color of the qr code(hexadecimal) #000000(black)
colorLight The light color of the qr code(hexadecimal) #FFFFFF(white)
