What are correction levels?

ECC (Error Correction Capacity) levels represent the amount of data that can be restored if the QR code symbol is smudged or damanged. There are four ECC(Error Correction Capacity) levels ranging from L (smallest) to H (best); L - Low : About 7% of codewords can be restored M - Medium : About 15% of codewords can be restored Q - Quartile : About 25% of codewords can be restored H - High : About 30% of codewords can be restored
